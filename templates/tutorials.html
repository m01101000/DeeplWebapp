EA 1: Image classification with ml5:
1. Select Task 1 from the menu or the corresponding field in the dashboard.

2. Select an image by clicking the button or drag and drop it into the application.

3. A few seconds later you will receive your classification results on the right.

4. Please decide whether the classification was correct or incorrect and save or discard your image.

5. All saved images can then be downloaded together with the results in an overview.
